Your Body's Framework and Movers

Bones give your body structure and support, while muscles help you move around!

Fun Fact: The smallest bone in your body is in your ear - it's called the stirrup and it's smaller than a grain of rice!

Fun Fact: You're Born with More Bones! Babies are born with about 270 bones, but as they grow, some fuse together, leaving 206 bones in adults.
