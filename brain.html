Your Body's Control Center

The brain is like a supercomputer that controls everything you do - from thinking and learning to moving and feeling!

Fun Fact: Your brain has about 100 billion neurons - that's more than all the stars in the Milky Way galaxy!

Fun Fact: Your brain generates enough electricity to power a small lightbulb (about 20 watts)!

Fun Fact: Your brain can store up to 2.5 petabytes of memory - that's like 3 million hours of TV shows!
